There are two ways to launch this app


###### Make Runner Clickable

Right-click on the folder containing your "[app-name].command", and click "New Terminal at Folder". Enter "chmod u+x [app-name].command". Then press enter. Close the window.


###### Run Once

Right-click on the folder containing your "[app-name].command", and click "New Terminal at Folder". Enter "sh [app-name].command". Then press enter. Close the window when done.


###### Tip

Tip: You can press tab as you enter the file name to auto complete it if it doesn't match other files at that point.
